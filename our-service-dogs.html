'Our Service Dogs'

5 stars on Amazon!

Come with Kaylee and her service dog, Riley, as they introduce 8 of their friends who all use service dogs to help them with their disabilities. Learn what you should do when you see a service dog. Kaylee, Riley, and all of their friends are based on real life kids and their hero service dogs. The appendix contains more information about each real-life team including pictures and quotes of what its like having a service dog.

This engaging, educational, inclusive children's book is perfect for any school, library or home.

Genre: Non-fiction
Audience: K-5th grade
Main body word count: 627
Appendix word count: apx. 575

Why I Chose to Write This Book

My daughter was born with Cerebral Palsy. She has an awesome service dog, Riley, to help her get around in the situations where a wheelchair and walker just don't cut it (stairs, the beach, a grassy hill... you get it). Turns out, there are loads of other children with special needs who's lives have all been enriched by having a service dog. "Our Service Dogs" features 8 of those other real-life teams. "Our Service Dogs" is a book about service dogs that shows the service dog community in its true light, as well as gives some tips for kids to know what to do when they see a service dog at school with a classmate or in public (hint: no petting!)
